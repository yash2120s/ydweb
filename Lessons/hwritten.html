What is Ethical Hacking or Ethical Hacker

  • Ethical means Legal Hacker means who do Hacking
  • It means the Hacking Which is done in legal way is known as Ethical Hacking & who do the Hacking In legal Way are Called Ethical Hackers
  • They do Penetration Testing & Cyber Security.
  • They Legally break into systems & servers.
  • They Secure Organizations.
  • They defeat bad hackers.
  • They Follow all Legal permissions & test the system.
  • They First ask Permission from the owner of website,system or app before testing it.
  • Their work is to find vurnablities or mistakes or error & solve it.

Types Of Hackers

There are three types of Hackers

  • Whitehat Hackers
  • Blackhat Hackers
  • Grayhat Hackers

Whitehat Hacker

  • They are also Known as Ethical Hacker.
  • They first get permission and then start Hacking.
  • They do all work Legally
  • We Have Already Discussed About It.

Blackhat Hacker

  • They are also known as UnEthical Hacker or Bad Hacker
  • They find vurnablities or mistakes or errors & use it to crash the system or getting information & sell it for money.
  • They do all illegal work
  • They dont ask permision from anyone before hacking their system , website or app
  • They are master of mind (man ke malik) They do what they want.

Grayhat Hacker

  • They can do good work also bad work also.
  • They can use their knowledge to secure any system or to crash or use it's information illegaly.
